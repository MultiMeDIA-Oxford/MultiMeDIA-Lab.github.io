Research Focus Areas

3D Cardiac Anatomy Reconstruction

Developing advanced algorithms for reconstructing detailed 3D models of cardiac structures from multimodal imaging data, enabling comprehensive anatomical analysis.

Chamber Geometry Analysis

Quantitative assessment of cardiac chamber dimensions, volumes, and geometric properties for diagnostic and prognostic applications.

Tissue Architecture Characterisation

Analysis of myocardial tissue composition, fiber orientation, and structural organization using advanced imaging techniques.

Morphological Feature Extraction

Automated extraction and quantification of morphological features for clinical decision support and research applications.

Current Projects

AI-driven Cardiac Segmentation

Machine learning models for automated segmentation of cardiac structures in medical imaging, improving accuracy and reducing analysis time.
