Research > Electrophysiological Research

Electrophysiological Heart Research

Analysis of electrical activity, arrhythmias, and cardiac conduction systems using advanced signal processing, machine learning, and computational modeling.

Research Focus Areas

ECG Signal Analysis

Advanced analysis of electrocardiographic signals including feature extraction, noise reduction, and automated interpretation using machine learning techniques.

Arrhythmia Detection and Classification

Development of AI-powered algorithms for real-time detection and classification of cardiac arrhythmias from various monitoring devices.

Cardiac Conduction System Mapping

Computational modeling and visualization of electrical conduction pathways in the heart using electrophysiological data.

Electrophysiological Modelling

Mathematical and computational models of cardiac electrical activity for understanding disease mechanisms and treatment planning.

Current Projects

AI-powered Arrhythmia Detection

Deep learning models for automated detection and classification of cardiac arrhythmias from wearable device data.
