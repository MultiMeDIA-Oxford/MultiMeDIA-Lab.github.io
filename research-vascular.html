Vascular Heart Research

Investigation of coronary arteries, blood flow dynamics, and vascular health through AI-enhanced imaging analysis and computational modeling.

Research Focus Areas

Coronary Artery Analysis

Comprehensive assessment of coronary artery anatomy, including vessel diameter, branching patterns, and pathological changes using advanced imaging techniques.

Blood Flow Dynamics Modelling

Computational fluid dynamics simulations and hemodynamic analysis to understand blood flow patterns and their clinical implications.

Vascular Tree Reconstruction

3D reconstruction of complete coronary vascular networks for detailed morphological and functional analysis.

Stenosis Detection and Quantification

Automated detection and precise quantification of arterial stenosis using machine learning and image analysis techniques.

Current Projects

AI-powered Vessel Segmentation

Deep learning algorithms for accurate and automated segmentation of coronary vessels from medical imaging data.
