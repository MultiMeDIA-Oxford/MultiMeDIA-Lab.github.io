Heart Research Focus Areas

Our lab specializes in comprehensive heart research across multiple dimensions

Structural Research

Advanced analysis of heart anatomy, chamber geometry, and tissue architecture using multimodal imaging techniques.

Vascular Research

Investigation of coronary arteries, blood flow dynamics, and vascular health through AI-enhanced imaging analysis.

Mechanical Research

Study of cardiac mechanics, contractility, strain analysis, and biomechanical properties of heart tissue.

Electrophysiological Research

Analysis of electrical activity, arrhythmias, and cardiac conduction systems using advanced signal processing.

Molecular Research

Integration of genomics, proteomics, and biomarker data with imaging for personalised cardiac medicine.

Advancing heart research through machine learning and multimodal medical data analysis
